SCOPE OF WORK

Our proposed scope includes the following key deliverables:

1. Environmental Review and Permitting
   - NEPA documentation and agency coordination
   - Environmental impact assessment
   - Regulatory compliance matrix

2. Preliminary Engineering (30% Design)
   - Alignment studies and alternatives analysis
   - Station preliminary design
   - Systems integration planning

3. Final Design (60-90% Design)
   - Construction documents
   - Technical specifications
   - Quality assurance review

4. Construction Support Services
   - RFI responses and submittal reviews
   - Site inspections and progress monitoring
   - Closeout documentation
